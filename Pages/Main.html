Liminal Escape

Escape liminality With Friends

Liminal Escape is a psychological horror/puzzle game that draws inspiration from the eerie world of the Backrooms, the unsettling SCP Foundation, and haunting real-world liminal spaces. Prepare to step into an unsettling journey where every corner holds mysteries, challenges, and chilling encounters

Immerse yourself in a true liminal experience, whether playing solo, with a friend, or with a group of up to six. Explore a labyrinthine environment filled with haunting atmospheres, enigmatic puzzles, and malevolent entities lurking in the shadows. With every step, uncover the secrets of the space and your purpose within it.
